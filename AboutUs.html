Swadesh Homes Foundation Inc is a New Jersey, USA based public charity that is exempt from from Federal Income Tax under IRS section 501(c)(3)

Swadesh Homes Foundation Inc was formed by founding members with roots in Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh, India. The goal of this organization is to assist the economically weaker section of the population by providing them with required assistance that will fulfil their dream of owning a properly designed home that provides basic features such as a bedroom, a kitchen, a toilet, a bathroom, running water and electricity. Swadesh Homes Foundation Inc has been partly inspired by the Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (PMAY), a scheme started by the Central Government of India to assist the economically weaker section of the population with assistance for home building. While working towards the goals defined by Swadesh Homes Foundation Inc, the organization will indirectly further the goal of PMAY as well.

To qualify as a beneficiary of the assistance program provided by Swadesh Homes Foundation Inc, candidates should fulfil the criteria outlined below:

  • The applicant should be a member of a family/household, seeking assistance with home building for that family
  • The land on which the home will be built should be owned (or legally inherited) by the applicant or one or more members of the same family that will be residing in that home
  • The combined annual household income of all members of the household should be less than Rs. 2 lakhs, before the family/household is selected as a qualified beneficiary
  • All adult members of the household must possess Aadhaar cards
  • The property (home to be built) must be co-owned by a female member of the family
  • The location of the property should fall under Varanasi district in Uttar Pradesh, which is where Swadesh Homes Foundation Inc is seeking to start with the goal of providing housing assistance
