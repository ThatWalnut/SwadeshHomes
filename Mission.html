MISSION STATEMENT

If you have a place you can call “Home”, you will have the will and courage to venture out and face any challenges life throws at you. As you face the challenges, you will have peace of mind that at the end of the day, you will be relaxing at the comfort of your home…

There are many in society who have not been as fortunate. Even though they have a piece of land, they don't have the resources or time to build a home on their property

Swadesh Homes Foundation is committed to helping the impoverished in India realize their dream of home ownership.

Our goal is to identify and target specifically those individuals and families who have a piece of land but have not been able to build a home on their property. Swadesh Homes will help them by building a house which provides the basic comforts that all human being deserve
